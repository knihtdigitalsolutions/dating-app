Key Feature Pillars

🔒 Secure SMS Authentication

Powered by Africa's Talking. Users log in seamlessly via dynamic one-time tokens sent directly to their phone numbers, eliminating weak password vulnerabilities.

🎙️ Audio & Video Streaming

Integrated premium communication suites (LiveKit WebRTC) allow matched users to trigger secure audio and video calls inside the app without revealing private contact details.

🧠 Context-Aware Matching

Leverages vector calculations to mathematically score personal profiles based on interests and behavior—moving past superficial card-swipes into real compatibility.

Bank-Grade Security & Privacy

Protecting user anonymity and financial transactions is baked into the foundation of the architecture rather than patched on afterward.

🛡️ Adaptive Threat Mitigation

The platform monitors over 60 distinct activity profiles. If automated behavior, scraping tools, or brute-force requests are flagged, the originating network signature is immediately banned for 24 hours.

  • Absolute Privacy: Precise user coordinates are never exposed. Distances between potential matches are computed mathematically on the server to prevent location spoofing.
  • Secured Transaction Pipelines: All payments bypass internal structures entirely via tokenized checkouts, ensuring full compliance and zero liability for cardholder data storage.
  • Data Ownership: Fully engineered with automated data-purge queues, enabling users to request instantaneous profile exports or permanent record deletions.

Business Scalability Overview

The solution is organized within an enterprise monorepo framework (Turborepo & pnpm workspaces). For you as a stakeholder, this means rapid time-to-market when introducing future modules (such as custom management dashboards or corporate advertising portals) since fundamental components are completely shared and standardized.
